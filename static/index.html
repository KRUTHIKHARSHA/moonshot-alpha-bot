Flow

From scan to execution
in under a second.

Every snipe runs through a deterministic pipeline — no guesswork, no hidden logic.

01

Token Scanner detects a candidate

Monitors DexScreener for new pairs passing liquidity, volume, and momentum thresholds in real time.

02

Rug Interceptor runs forensic checks

Mint authority, freeze authority, holder concentration, and honeypot analysis run concurrently via Solana RPC + DexScreener.

03

Risk score gates the sniper

Tokens above the configured risk threshold are rejected before any capital is committed. Only clean tokens reach the execution step.

04

Swap Engine fetches best route & submits via Jito

Jupiter Aggregator finds the optimal route. The transaction is bundled and sent to the Jito block-engine for MEV-protected, priority landing.

05

Ghost Manager monitors the open position

Watches price every 10 seconds server-side. Auto-sells at your take-profit or stop-loss — even if the bot is offline.
